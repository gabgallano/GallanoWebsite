Westpoint History

Since its inception, Westpoint has a rich history, serving as a meeting place for businessmen and a popular spot for locals. However, it struggled to compete with modern entertainment and fell into disrepair.

Recently, passionate locals have restored the hall, reviving its lively atmosphere and making it a place for people to gather and enjoy a game of pool.
